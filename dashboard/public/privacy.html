Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the xNico Discord bot ("the Bot") and its web dashboard ("the Dashboard") collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the Service you consent to the practices described here.

1. Data we collect

  • Discord identifiers — user, server, channel, and role IDs needed to provide features (for example moderation logs, leveling, and economy balances).
  • Authentication data — when you sign in to the Dashboard via Discord OAuth2, we receive your basic profile and the list of servers you can manage. A signed session token (JWT) is stored in your browser to keep you logged in.
  • Configuration you provide — module settings, message templates, and similar content you enter in the Dashboard or via bot commands.
  • Usage counters — aggregate activity such as message and command counts used for leveling and analytics.

We do not intentionally collect the contents of your direct messages, and we do not sell your data.

2. How we use data

Data is used solely to operate the Service: to run the features you enable, to keep the Dashboard and the Bot in sync, to enforce premium entitlements, and to display analytics back to you.

3. Storage & retention

Configuration and counters are stored in the Service's database for as long as the Bot remains in your server or until the related data is deleted. Browser session tokens expire automatically. Removing the Bot from a server stops further collection for that server.

4. Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except as required to run the Service (for example the hosting and database provider) or where required by law. Communication with Discord is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.

5. Your choices

  • Sign out of the Dashboard at any time to clear your session.
  • Remove the Bot from a server to stop data collection for it.
  • Request deletion of your stored data through our support server.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data, including hashed credentials and signed session tokens. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, reach us in our support server.
